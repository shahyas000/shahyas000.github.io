Tennis Tourament!

Information:

This is for High School students ages 14-18. This is as team event. Each team will have 7 players. 3 singles players and 2 doubles teams. This will be consisting of a total of 56 players. Teams will be randomized. Each team will choose individual positioning and pairings. This is a whole day event.

More details:

  • It is Saturday August 21st
  • At HSE High School Tennis courts
  • Each team needs 7 people
  • There will be a $15 fee
  • It will be a single elimination tournament
  • The winning team will get $50 per player and 2nd place will get $25 per person

Rules:

  1. Make fair calls.If need of line judge talk to officals.
  2. Parents should not be coaching
  3. Lineups cannot change when playing diffrent teams
  4. Point penalties are in effect so no saying the lords name in vain, cursing, or abuse to equiment. You will first loose a point, then a game, and finally match.
  5. Each game will be a pro set of 8 games. If score is 7-7, play 2 more games. When score is 8-8 play a tiebreaker to 10. If you need help on how to play a tie breaker call over an offical.
